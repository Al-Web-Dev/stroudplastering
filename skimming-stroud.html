What is Plaster Skimming?

Skimming is the process of applying a thin coat of finishing plaster over existing walls or ceilings to create a smooth, paint-ready surface. It’s ideal for covering imperfections, old textures, or damaged plaster.

When Do You Need Skimming?

  • • After removing wallpaper
  • • Covering artex ceilings
  • • Repairing damaged plaster
  • • Before painting or decorating
  • • Renovation or home upgrades
